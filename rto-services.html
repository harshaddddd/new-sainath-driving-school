Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does it take to get a Learner's License in Pune? +
With our assistance, the LL process typically takes 7‚Äì10 days from the day you apply. We help with online slot booking and the written test is usually cleared on the same day at the RTO.
Can I apply for a Permanent License without a Learner's License? +
No. The Learner's License is mandatory and must be at least 30 days old before you can apply for a Permanent DL. We'll guide you through the LL first, then DL.
Do you accompany students to the RTO for the practical test? +
Yes. Our trainers prepare you extensively for the test route and we support you on test day with coaching on what to expect, how to handle the test vehicle, and the exact maneuvers required.
What is the minimum age to apply for a driving license in India? +
You must be at least 16 years old for a Gearless 2-Wheeler (with parent consent) and 18 years for a Car or Geared 2-Wheeler. We can guide you based on your age and vehicle type.
My license expired. Can you help with renewal? +
Absolutely. We regularly assist with license renewals. If expired within 5 years, the process is straightforward. Beyond 5 years, there may be additional requirements. Call us to assess your situation.
